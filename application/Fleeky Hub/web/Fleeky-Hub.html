    Staffing Management

    Staff Acquisition:

    For the JIT Project, the project staff will consist of three teams; the Project Management Team, Development Team, and Quality Team. The planning stage is where the project team would outsource and contract members or external entities that would aid the project team to success. The Project Manager will negotiate with the functional leaders in the team to identify required skills and resources that must be allocated in accordance with the project management structure. All staff and resources must be approved by the relevant functional leader before the staff and resources may begin any project work. The project team will share location of the project to ensure the consistency throughout the whole project.

    Resource Calendars:

    The JIT Project development will last for ten months including the planning, quality testing, and closeout documents. All resources must be identified and evaluated before the project can start. The resources histogram illustrates that the project team are split into three teams mainly Project Management, Development, and Quality team. They are required to perform between specific times to complete a task assigned to the team which explains the shift in the required hours each month. The Project Management Team required to perform 8 hours a day which will accumulate to 185 hours per month within the one to two months and nine to ten months of JIT Project. The job of Project Management is to guide the other teams on the tasks assigned to them.

    The Project Development Team is required to perform 8 hours a day and 185 hours per month within the three months to eight months of JIT Project Development since within this time they are the one developing the JIT Project. After the required months the required hours will begin to lessen. The Quality Management team has the most inconsistent required of hours because they need an output first before performing their task. In the first three months of the JIT Project they don’t have any hours of required at all since they do not have any work assigned to them yet. After three months, they are required to perform at least four to five hours of time. During the last two months, the required time begins to increase.

    =Risk Management Plan=

    Introduction

    The JIT Project Management anticipated that risk and uncertainty of event that comes along with developing new and unique project. That said, the JIT Project Management takes advantage of the anticipated results by creating Risk Management Plan. The purpose of the Risk Management Plan is to establish the framework in which the JIT Project team will identify risk and developing strategies to counter act or avoid those risk. However, before risk can be identified and managed, there are preliminary project elements which must be completed. These elements are outlined in the risk management approach.

    This project is considered a high-risk project as its function is to be the main transaction for the company of MC Manabat. The project’s function involves money transaction and confidential information transfer between the process of the JIT Project’s website, so the security of the website must be tested and check regularly to avoid implications within the business.

    Before risk management begins it is imperative that a foundation is established for providing structured project information, thus, the following project elements were completed and defined prior to developing this Risk Management Plan:

        Scope, Schedule, and Cost Management Plan must first be established to include:

    Top Three Risks

    The top three high probability and high impact risks to The JIT Project are:

    Security Vulnerabilities

    Due to the Internet of things gradually growing more of the securities are breach and it is important to the JIT Project especially dealing with money transaction to prevent or create a mitigation plan when event happens. The project manager will ensure that the risk of prevent or mitigate authorized person to hi-jack or acquire confidential information in the JIT Project.

    Time Management

    Due to the uncertainty of event that the Project Management assumes that will happen throughout the Project that will result in delaying of the project schedule as well as wasting resources for the JIT Project requires a plan of action of the process that will happen in case a problem event occurs. The Project Manager will mitigate the risk for the problems that will happen in the future and will have a back-up plan a ready.

    Consistency of the Project

    Due to ineffective methodology and practice can lead the Project Management to have a risk in the Consistency of the project that will result in unintentional change of plans in the Project that will lead a dissatisfaction and disappointment of the Project Stakeholders and Client of the project. The project manager will mitigate the risk and it will require the members to adjust so that the Project Management will use as the standard practice.

    Risk Management Approach

    The risk analysis regarding information systems that JIT Project Management have taken approach to manage risk for this project which the Project Management identified and ranked the various risks. The Project Team prioritize the highest impact risk were added to project schedule to ensure that the risk would minimize its probability of becoming a problem as well as taking the necessary precaution to implement the mitigation response plan at the appropriate time. Team leaders assigned in the risk management will inform status updates on their assigned risks and must be updated weekly by having the meetings with the project manager, but only when the meetings include their risk’s planned timeframe. Before the completion of the project, during closing process, the project manager will analyze the risk as well as the process to it. Based on the analysis, the project manager will identify any improvements that can be added to the risk management process for the Project Client to know as part of the maintenance of the website after the turnover has happened.

    Risk Identification

    For the JIT Project, risk analysis method used by the Project team to identify risk is Qualitative Risk Analysis. The risk assessment meeting representatives discuss and create scenarios that outline the potential threats to the business and rank these threats according to the severity of the potential problems if it occurred. The procedure writing scenarios for the major risk, estimating the effects of event, if it happened, and creating a counter measures and safeguards against the event.

    Simulation Test

    One Security Analyst (SA) is involved in the project. The SA will simulate cyber-attack against the JIT Project to test the website whether the penetration of the SA will be successful, or the website would safely be unaffected by the attack. The result will be documented, and the JIT Project will be improved based on the comments and suggestion of the SA.

    Reserve Funds

    A risk assessment meeting discussing allocating resources for uncertainty of events and must be only used when occurrence of the problem arises in the project management.

    Daily Team Meeting

    Each team leader must discuss the status of their members about work assigned to them, what are the issues that they encounter, what solutions they need to work on, and what they will do for the day.

    Risk Assessment Meeting

    A risk assessment meeting was held with key team leaders and stakeholders. The risks identified during this meeting were added to the project plan and Risk Table.

    Historical Review of Similar Projects

    The project team reviewed the history of similar projects to determine the most common risks and the strategies used to mitigate those risks.

    Risk Qualification and Prioritization

    To determine the severity of the risk that will be identified by the team, a probability and impact was used as metric to the assigned risk. This process allowed the team leaders and project manager to prioritize risk based on the severity of the effect that the risk would have on the project. The project manager utilized a probability-impact matric to manage the team leader in moving each risk to the appropriate place on the table. Once the risks were assigned a probability and impact and placed in the appropriate position on the table, documenting the finished website and the project manager moved the process on to the next step: risk mitigation/avoidance planning.

    Risk Monitoring

    The project schedules an assigned team member for each risk. During the daily meeting between leaders and members is assigned for each risk. The meeting will discuss the status of their work and as well as the risk associated to it. There will be a discussion of risk if the team member is assigned to do the work module with risk probability to it. Risk monitoring will be a continuous process throughout the life of the JIT Project. As risk approach on the JIT project the project manager will check the team leader’s status report of risk management of their members as well as the leaders with necessary status updates which include the risk status, identification of trigger conditions, and the documentation of the risk response.

    Risk Mitigation and Avoidance

    The project manager ensures to aid team leader in developing and improving responses to each identified risk. As more risk are identified, if the risk is qualified based on the metric established, the team has to respond to develop avoidance and mitigation strategies to the risk. The identified risk will be also added to the risk register or in the table and the project plan to ensure that they are monitored when the work involves those risk.

    The risk for the JIT Project will be managed and controlled within the constraints of schedule, authority, and budget cost. All identified risk will be analyzed to determine how the risk will impact the time, scope, cost. The project manager with the assistance of the project team experts will determine the efficient way to deal with each risk to ensure that everything goes according to plan. There will be cases that it will be necessary to allow the project to extend its budget cost. If only necessary to allow the JIT Project for more resources to meet the deadline and requirements of the JIT project.

    Risk Register

    The Risk Register is a log or list of all identified risk of the JIT Project. The list consists of risk description of their probability and impact, the category they belong to, mitigation strategy, and what event would trigger them. The risk register was identified in the project management meeting led by the project manager. During this meeting, the project team identified that there is a need of creating a risk management plan that the project team categorized each risk. In addition, the team assigned each risk will prioritize according to the probability and impact of the risk it could potentially have. The risk register also describes the mitigation strategy on each risk.

    The identified risk in the risk register, each risk has been classified and added to the project plan. When the work module contains a risk—prior to when the risk is going to occur—the project manager will assign a leader to ensure adherence to the agreed mitigation strategy. Each assigned leader will provide the status of the risk relevant to their group in their daily project meeting.

    =Communication Management Plan=

    Introductions

    The reason of creating a Communications Management Plan is for the project team to determine what are the communication requirements for the project and how information will be delivered. Communications Management Plan will be the one setting the scheme for the communications of the project, it will serve as a guide for the project team members throughout the duration of the project and it will only be changed or updated when needed. It also determines and describe the roles of persons involved in the project.

    Communication Management Approach

    The project manager will be the one responsible in ensuring effective communications on this project. The project manager will be allocating most of his time looking out for the project and reporting the performance of the project to the project team members and stakeholders, he will also be responsible organizing meeting, creating the project plan, constructing and reading emails, meeting with his project team members and managing the work being performed by them.

    By having a good communication management approach a lot of project management problems can be prevented.

    Communication Management Constraints

    Every project is no exception to limitation and constraints because they must be within the scope of the project and comply with its budget, scheduling and resource necessity. So the project planning and documentation is also limited and constrained. There must be an administrative or organizational policy requirements that must be followed as part of communications management and these constraints must be clearly discussed to all the project stakeholders and make sure that all of them have understood.

    All activities in the project communication will appear within the project’s approved budget, schedule and resource allocations. The one who will be responsible in ensuring that all communication activities are performed by the project team without external resources that may affect the budget and exceed the authorized budget is the project manager.
